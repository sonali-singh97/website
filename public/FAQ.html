Q. Do I have to register for an event organized by DSC if I am already a chapter member?

A. Yes, you have to register for every event organized by DSC.

Q. I only know C++. Can I still get useful stuff in DSC ?

A. Yes, you will get to learn a lot of useful stuff in DSC such as Data Structures and Algorithms, whose application requires good knowledge in one programming language. Apart from that, we have Web Development, Machine Learning and other tech stacks which are very very useful in todayâ€™s time.

Q. Will I get access to events without registering an event before it gets started?

A. No, to join an event you have to register for it before it begins.

Q. Need to fill the form everytime we join an event ?

A. After registering for at least one event at DSC, your form will be prefilled (that is, main details such as name, DOB, interests) for all the future events.

Q. Whether the certificates will be provided for every tech workshop?

A. Yes, you will be getting certificates for attending a series of sessions.

Q. Whether DSC will help in our academia syllabus?

A. We have Data Structures and Algorithms as one of our domains in DSC which will help you study well for the academia syllabus. Also, companies require proficiency in DSA hence it will help you there as well.

Q. Whether DSC will help in some fixed particular tech stack or it can help in others techs too in which I specialized in?

A. DSC will help each and everyone in whatsoever tech stack they want to specialize in. You can become a master in one, two or all the domains depending on the amount of hard work and time you want to give it.

Q. Is it for only CSE and ECE students only?

A. No, DSC is not limited to any particular branch. The club is open for students of all branches.

Q. What are the pre requirements to be a member of DSC, whether I should have basic knowledge in programming or not?

A. No, as such there are no predefined requirements to join DSC. We only want you to be dedicated and sincere throughout the entire journey.
